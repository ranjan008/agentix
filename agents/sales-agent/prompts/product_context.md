## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Primary buyer personas
| Persona | Title examples | Company size | Pain they feel |
|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue leader | VP Sales, CRO, Head of Sales | 50–2,000 employees | Pipeline visibility, forecast accuracy, rep ramp time |
| RevOps lead | Head of RevOps, Sales Ops Manager | 100–5,000 employees | Tool sprawl, data quality, process automation |
| Founder/CEO | CEO, Co-founder | 10–200 employees | Scaling sales without headcount |

### Firmographic filters
- Industry: SaaS, Tech, Professional Services, Financial Services
- Funding: Series A–D or revenue-funded with $5M+ ARR
- Geography: North America, UK, DACH, ANZ
- Tech signals: Uses Salesforce or HubSpot (indicates sales maturity)

### Negative ICP (do NOT target)
- Pure consumer products (B2C)
- Government or non-profit (long cycles, no budget authority)
- Sub-10 person companies (not yet ready for our solution)
- Competitors' employees (obviously)

## Key value propositions by persona

### For VP Sales / CRO
- Cut time-to-first-meaningful-conversation by 40%
- Real-time pipeline health scores to stop surprises at quarter end
- AI coaching that ramps new reps 2× faster

### For RevOps
- Single source of truth across CRM, SEP, and conversation intelligence
- Automated data enrichment — no more manual Salesforce hygiene
- Custom workflow builder without engineering tickets

### For CEO / Founder
- Replace 2 SDR headcount with one AI-assisted rep
- Board-ready pipeline reporting in one click
- Works alongside your existing stack (no rip-and-replace)

## Competitive differentiators
- Deploys in days, not months (no professional services required)
- Model trained on 10M+ B2B conversations — not a generic LLM wrapper
- SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant (important for enterprise)

## Common objections and how to handle them
| Objection | Response angle |
|---|---|
| "We already have [competitor]" | Acknowledge, then ask about the specific gap that still exists |
| "Not the right time / budget" | Tie to an upcoming event (QBR, hiring push, new territory) |
| "Send me more info" | Offer a 15-min live demo instead — info decks don't close deals |
| "We're happy with our current process" | Reference a similar company that felt the same, then had a trigger event |
